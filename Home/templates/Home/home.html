    Our Mission

    We aim to provide students with the opportunity to learn more about Computer science and prepare them for jobs in the future through lectures, competitions and lots of googling.

    Club Activites

    ACSL

    The American Computer Science League (ACSL) competition challenges students in programming and problem-solving. Last year, we participated in the classroom division and placed first in Oregon in the team contest. We had two members tie for the highest individual scores in the state while another member earned a silver medal in the world finals.

    Contests

    The George Fox University Programming Contest is a state-wide coding contest run by George Fox University. We had two teams in the contest. Our teams, Hackermans and python.win(True) won 1st and 2nd place respectively in division 3. Hackermans ended up placing 3rd across all divisions.
